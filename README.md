REDU
=================================

REDU stands for REkreasi eDUkasi in Bahasa. REDU is a platform for people to share their experiences on travelling or tourism, especially on its educational aspect. The purpose of REDU is to increase awareness of travelling destinations that offer educational aspects along with recreational aspects.
